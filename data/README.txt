Dataset compiled and used by Bione et al. (2024).

Description: 

DT - Sonic Transit Time (μs/ft)
CALI - Caliper (in)
RESD - Deep Resistivity (ohm/m)
RHOB - Density (g/cm3)
GR - Gamma Ray (gAPI)
SP - Spontaneous Potential (mV)
DEPT - Measured depth (m)
TOC - Measured Total organic carbon (wt%)
S1 - Rock Eval S1 peak.
S2 - Rock Eval S2 peak.
S3 - Rock Eval S3 peak.
Tmax - Rock Eval maximum temperature (°C).
WELL - Label-encoded well ID.
NPHI - Neutron porosity (%).
PEF - Photoeletric factor (b/e).
LAT - Latitude (decimal degrees).
LON - Longitude (decimal degrees).
Location - Well basin location. 0 = onshore; 1 = offshore.



Reference: 
BIONE F.R.A. et al. Estimating total organic carbon of potential source rocks in the Espírito Santo Basin, SE Brazil, using XGBoost. Marine and Petroleum Geology, v. 162, 106765, 2024. 
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.marpetgeo.2024.106765
